# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 1, 2025

## Introduction

QuickToolFlow ("we," "our," "us") operates quicktoolflow.com. This policy describes how we handle your information.

## Data Processing

**All tool processing happens locally in your browser.** The text, code, passwords, colors, and any other data you enter into our tools are processed entirely on your device using client-side JavaScript. This data is never transmitted to, stored on, or processed by our servers.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected

We may collect non-personally-identifiable information: browser type, operating system, pages visited, referring website, and general geographic region.

### Contact Form

If you contact us via our form, we collect the information you voluntarily provide (name, email, message) solely to respond to your inquiry.

## Advertising

We use Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your visits. You may opt out via [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

## Cookies

- **Essential cookies:** Required for basic site functionality.
- **Advertising cookies:** Used by Google AdSense for ad personalization.
