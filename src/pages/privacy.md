# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 4, 2026

## Introduction

QuickToolFlow ("we," "our," "us") operates quicktoolflow.com. This policy describes how we handle your information.

## Data Processing

**All tool processing happens locally in your browser.** The text, code, passwords, colors, and any other data you enter into our tools are processed entirely on your device using client-side JavaScript. This data is never transmitted to, stored on, or processed by our servers.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected

We may collect non-personally-identifiable information such as browser type, operating system, pages visited, referring website, and general geographic region through analytics and server logs.

### Contact Form

If you contact us via our form, we collect the information you voluntarily provide (name, email, message) solely to respond to your inquiry.

## Advertising

We may use Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads, measure ad performance, and personalize ads depending on your settings and applicable law. You may opt out via [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

## Cookies

- **Essential cookies:** Required for basic site functionality.
- **Analytics cookies:** Used to understand aggregate site usage.
- **Advertising cookies:** Used by Google AdSense for ad personalization.
